Our Story

Born from a Small Town Dream

What happens when childhood wishes refuse to stay wishes?

Growing up in Nakrekal — a warm, close-knit town in the heart of Telangana — a group of best friends shared everything: school adventures, summer afternoons, and one quiet longing. The kind of resort getaways, poolside summers, and grand celebration venues that always seemed to exist only in bigger cities, just out of reach for families like theirs.

Years passed. Each of them built their own journey. But that longing never left.

So they came back together — not just as friends, but as founders — with one purpose: to build the experience they once had to miss, right inside their own town. So that no child in Nakrekal would grow up envying what the big cities have. So that every wedding could be magical, every birthday unforgettable, every summer afternoon worth diving into.

"We didn't want the next generation to miss what we missed. That's the whole reason Athidhi exists."

Nakrekal, Telangana Founded by friends Built for the community
